Chunyi Wang is currently a graduate Research Assistant in Dong Lab, where she focuses on drug repurposing for neurological disorders. She is an MPH candidate in Biostatistics at the Yale School of Public Health (expected May 2026), where she has developed strong expertise in machine learning, statistical modeling, and large language models. In the lab, Chunyi leads analyses on the PPMI and PDBP datasets, investigating the association between ARBs (angiotensin receptor blockers) and Parkinson's disease dementia (PDD). Outside of research, Chunyi enjoys listening to music, skiing, and exploring new restaurants.
